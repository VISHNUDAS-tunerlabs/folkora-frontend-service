# Frontend Experience Flow

## Overview

Folkora is a curated travel discovery platform designed to feel personal, calm, and intentional.

Unlike traditional travel websites, users should not be immediately presented with search bars, filters, or destination listings.

Every visit should begin with a lightweight onboarding experience that helps Folkora understand the user's current mindset and travel preferences before presenting recommendations.

The onboarding experience should feel conversational rather than transactional.

The objective is to make users feel that Folkora understands them before recommending journeys.

# Screen 1 — Welcome Experience

## Purpose

Introduce Folkora and establish the emotional tone of the product.


## User Feeling

The user should feel:

* Curious
* Relaxed
* Inspired

## Content Direction

Examples:

> Travel isn't about destinations.
>
> It's about how you want to feel.

or

> Discover journeys curated for your mood, not your search query.

## UI Characteristics

* Minimal interface
* Large typography
* Subtle motion
* Elegant illustrations or imagery
* Single primary action

## Goal

Move users into the personalization journey.

# Screen 2 — Mood Collection

## Purpose

Understand how the user feels right now.

The focus is not demographic data.

The focus is emotional context.

## Example Mood Options

* I need a break
* I want adventure
* I want to slow down
* I want to reconnect
* I want something unexpected
* I need inspiration
* I want to escape routine

## UI Characteristics

* Card selection
* Smooth transitions
* Playful but refined interactions

## Goal

Capture the user's current travel intent.
